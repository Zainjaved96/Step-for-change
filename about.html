WHO ARE WE?

STEP FOR CHANGE

Pakistan's inadequate education system inspired us to establish STEP for Change. Our objective is to equip final year college students with the appropriate technical and non-technical skills to thrive in the competitive job market and break the cycle of poverty, one step at a time.

What We Do?

Investing in the Future of Pakistan

Through our comprehensive programs, we offer training in a variety of in-demand technical roles, such as web development, app development, and full-stack JavaScript. Our program is designed to provide a holistic learning experience that includes both technical and soft skills training, such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving.

Our Impact

The need of the hour

We measure our success by the impact we have on the lives of our students. Since our inception, we have successfully trained and placed several students in high-paying jobs, providing them with financial stability and a pathway to a better future.
