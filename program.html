Flagship Program - (9 months) LIVE

For the past three years, our flagship program has been training final year computer science students for a 9-month rigorous program in Full-Stack development. This program is divided into three equal phases. The first phase is a three-month basic training module, followed by a rotation in Front-end, Back-end, and QA in the second phase. The third phase is a final placement in the department of their choice.

  • Intensive training in technical skills, such as web development, app development, full stack javascript, HTML, etc.
  • Soft skills training, including communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and time management.
  • Mock interviews, resume building, and job search strategies to prepare students for employment.
  • Career counselling and job placement assistance to help graduates find suitable employment opportunities.
  • Regular assessments and evaluations to track progress and ensure students are meeting program requirements.

    • By including the below technical skills in our flagship program,STEP can equip its students with the latest tools and technologies that are in demand in the market and help them secure well-paying jobs in the emerging tech industry.
