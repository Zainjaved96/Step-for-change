First step towards a successful career!

Are you a final year student looking to gain practical skills and kick-start your career? Look no further than STEP's flagship program. You'll work on live projects in three phases, earning certificates along the way. Finish the final phase and unlock job opportunities through our partner network. Gain the edge you need to launch your career.

Eligibility Criteria:

To be eligible for our program, you must be a final year enrolled student with a strong motivation to learn new skills and become industry-ready. Our programs are completely free for all students, with no charges at any stage.

Recruitment Process:

Our recruitment process is simple and straightforward, with just three steps:

01

Application Form

Start by filling out our simple application form with a few details about yourself, your education background and overall motivation.

02

Aptitude Test

Once you've submitted your application, you will be invited to take an aptitude test to assess your potential and readiness for the program.

03

Selection

Our selection process is based on your overall motivation, academic performance, and the results of your aptitude test. Once you're selected, you'll be notified and invited to join our program.

Don't miss this opportunity to enhance your skills. Apply now and join the hundreds of successful STEP graduates who are making a difference in their communities and beyond.
